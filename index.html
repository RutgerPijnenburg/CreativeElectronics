Creative Electronics

For this course we worked a lot with electronics, as the name suggests, the taught me the basics about how electrical components work and how to use them in a system, we had to make a multitude of reports and lastly made a prototype to showcase what we had learned in the course. In this course I progressed in the making of reports, becoming better in writing reports, I also got to work on some creativity and aesthetics in the form of the covers I made for these reports, lastly the course also allowed me to develop in technology and realisation as I am now able to make prototypes that show interaction better. Our teamwork mostly went well, we were able to clearly divide tasks, each of us working on what we were good at, while still allowing improvement, the only thing that went less well was my fault, I was sometimes stressing because my teammate was doing things last minute, this is something I do more often, as I usually like to submit well before the deadline. For next time I can see two courses of action, the first would be just trusting that the work will be done, and the other would be saying my opinion, this situation will occur again which is why I have to choose, in my opinion the second is better, however I am currently not able to say my mind like that, so for now I will use the first method and I will work on my communication in the meantime.
